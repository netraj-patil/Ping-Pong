How to play


This is a ping pong game and it can be played by only one player at a time. The player has to play versus computer. There are tuo paddles on right and left side which help in playing the game.
The left paddle is yours and the right one is of computer's. Press the play button to play the game. Once pressed, the ball will start moving towards computer's paddle at first. you can control your paddle by moving your mouse in the gaming area.
In case you felt like pausing the game you can do it by pressing the pause button above. There is also a reset button, which will reset yours and computer's score.
If you miss hitting the ball computer's score will increase and ball's position will again reset to original. If computer miss the ball, your score will increase and the ball then will come to you first, after resetting its position.
